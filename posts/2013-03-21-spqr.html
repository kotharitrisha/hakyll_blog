Fun fact 1

Posted in: fun.

Australia has the kangaroo and emu bird on all of its stamps.

Apart from being the national animal and national bird, the kangaroo and the emu bird are the only animal and bird which cannot walk backwards.

Hence, if Australia signs a deal, it will not go backwards.
